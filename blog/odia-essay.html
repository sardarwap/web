Tips to Write a Scoring Essay

Downloading the PDF is the first step. To score 10/10 in your exam, follow this structure:

  • Upakrama (Introduction): Start with a famous quote or a strong definition of the topic.
  • Bisaya Bastu (Body): Divide your main content into 2-3 paragraphs. Do not write one huge block of text.
  • Upasamhara (Conclusion): End with a positive note or your personal opinion on the subject.
