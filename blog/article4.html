Math Anxiety: Why You Have It and How to Cure It

Math Dept.

Nov 25, 2025 • 4 min read

Does your heart beat faster when you see a Trigonometry problem? Do you go blank during exams even if you studied? This is called "Math Anxiety," and it affects 40% of students.

Math is a Language, Not a Monster

The biggest mistake students make is trying to memorize math steps like they memorize history dates. You cannot memorize math. You must understand the "Why". Why does `(a+b)^2 = a^2 + b^2 + 2ab`? Once you understand the logic, the fear disappears.

The "Daily 3" Rule

To cure this fear, solve just 3 problems a day. Not 30. Just 3. Do this every day for a month. Consistency beats intensity.
