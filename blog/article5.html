Why "Previous Year Papers" Are Gold for Board Exams

EduOdia Team

Nov 28, 2025 • 4 min read

If you have only 30 days left for your exam, stop reading the textbook. Start solving Previous Year Questions (PYQs). Here is why PYQs are the secret weapon of every topper.

1. The 80/20 Rule

In every exam board, 80% of the questions come from 20% of the syllabus. The board repeats concepts every year. By solving the last 5 years of papers, you identify these "Favorite Topics" of the examiners.

2. Time Management

Knowing the answer is not enough. You must write it within 3 hours. Solving papers gives you a reality check on your writing speed.
