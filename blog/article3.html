Top 5 High-Paying Career Options After Class 12 Science

Career Counselor

Nov 22, 2025 • 6 min read

Many students in Odisha think that Engineering (B.Tech) and Medicine (MBBS) are the only two options after Science. While these are great, the world is changing. Here are 5 modern career paths that offer high salaries and great growth.

1. Data Science & AI

Data is the new oil. Companies need people who can analyze numbers and predict trends. You can pursue a B.Sc in Data Science or Computer Science. The starting salary is often higher than traditional engineering roles.

2. Architecture

If you are creative and good at physics/math, Architecture is a beautiful blend of art and engineering. The NATA exam is your gateway to top colleges.

3. Merchant Navy

For those who love the sea and want to travel the world while earning tax-free income, the Merchant Navy is an exciting option. Look for B.Sc Nautical Science courses.
