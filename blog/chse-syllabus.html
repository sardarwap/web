How to Download from CHSE Website?

You can also download the syllabus directly from the official website by following these steps:

  • Visit the official site: chseodisha.nic.in.
  • Go to the "Syllabus" section in the main menu.
  • Select your stream (Arts/Science/Commerce).
  • Click on the PDF icon to save it to your mobile or computer.
