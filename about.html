About EduOdia

Welcome to EduOdia.in, Odisha's leading educational portal dedicated to students, teachers, and parents. We understand the challenges students face in finding organized, high-quality study materials. Our mission is to bridge this gap by providing a comprehensive digital library.

Whether you are a student of Class 1 or preparing for your CHSE +2 Board Exams, EduOdia is your one-stop destination for Textbooks, Syllabus updates, Exam Guides, and Educational News.

What We Provide

Textbooks

Complete PDF collection of BSE Odisha & CHSE books from Class 1 to 12.

Exam Resources

Previous year question papers, model papers, and syllabus updates.

Smart Tools

Free utilities like Image Resizer and Odia OCR to help with online forms.
